Rüppell's

Vulture

The Rüppell's Vulture is the highest-flying bird ever ever recorded ,soaring up to 37,000 feet. Today, habitat loss and accidental poisoning have wiped over 97% of their wild poplulation They are on the brink of extinction-and they need protection.
